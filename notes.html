2026-04-30

A simple practice (sample)

Pause. Notice what is present. Thoughts, sensations, and emotions appear. Ask gently: What is aware of this?

Then: Is the aware presence changing, or are the objects changing? Rest as the knowing.

2026-04-29

Neti Neti (sample)

Whatever can be pointed to is not “I”. Whatever is seen is not the Seer. Keep discarding what is objective until silence remains.
