What this is

Jnana is a personal notebook for self-inquiry and spiritual reflections. It’s not meant to be perfect or final— only sincere and updated as understanding deepens.

What you’ll find here

  • Short notes (sometimes only a few lines)
  • Reflections on inquiry and awareness
  • Gita-inspired reminders for daily life (sample)

How to use it

If something resonates, sit with it quietly. If it doesn’t, leave it. Keep returning to direct seeing.

“Not this, not this.” — Neti Neti
